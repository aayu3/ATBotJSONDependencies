Introduction

This is a basic reroll guide for Action Taimanin. Rerolling in this game isn't particularly important as the game is very F2P friendly and you can roll discount gacha fairly often. The main goal of rerolling is to get supporters, weapons are less important unless you plan to whale, as an SLVL 5 Arena or Event weapon will be far better than an SLVL 1-2 Gacha UR weapon. We also won't be covering Limited Gacha, as the supporters can be a hit or miss, for example Rinko and Murasaki were quite good, while Kirara is a bit lackluster in comparison. If you are rerolling and have questions about the limited gacha, feel free to ask in the discord.

Supporters

We will only be covering supporters currently avalible in the Standard Gacha, and from an F2P players point of view, this means we will assume you only get 1 copy for the SLvl for passive skills.

Assist

All of the green supporters can be good, Fujibayashi Yuno is the primary target for PvP, and Kasahara Tatsumi can be quite decent. You can redeem Tekkain Kaworu from the rank 10 ticket so she is not as important to roll for.

  • PvE
    • Tekkain Kaworu

      Tekkain Kaworu is one of the best main assist supporters for PvE in the game, her active skill is guaranteed to hit all enemies on the screen, with a large amount of damage, stun, as well as one of the shorter cooldowns. Her passive skill is also decent, just providing a damage buff to critical hits.

    • Yuri Hisui

      Yuri Hisui is one of the most interesting supporters in the game. She provides a sort of glass cannon strategy where you can provide an extremely powerful 200% damage buff for 10 seconds, every 48 seconds. Unfortunately, this only really works with Emily, and Hisui is only really useful if you plan to get Emily. In addition, other just passive damage boosters tend to be better.

  • PvP
    • Fujibayashi Yuno

      Fujibayashi Yuno is the best main assist supporters for PvP, she provides invulnerability on skill, which is the current meta for PvP. Only one copy is needed to max out invulnerability time, so if you get multiple, do NOT fuse them together.

  • Both
    • Kasahara Tatsumi

      Kasahara Tatsumi is pretty decent for both PvE and PvP. She has an extremely good passive, just giving additional damage against Demons and Humans. Her lead skill is also quite good on a decent cooldown. The main utility is disarming which prevents the opponent from attacking for up to 8 seconds, providing a pseudo-invulnerability. Her main shortcoming is the short-mediumish range of the attack

Protect

None of the blue supporters in the current pool are particularly worth going for. Their lead skills aren't very good, and their passive skills can be done by SR supporters. The only supporter worth getting is Fuuma Amane, who you can get from the rank 10 choice ticket.
