Introduction

This is a basic reroll guide for Action Taimanin. Rerolling in this game isn't particularly important as the game is very F2P friendly and you can roll discount gacha fairly often. The main goal of rerolling is to get supporters, weapons are less important unless you plan to whale, as an SLVL 5 Arena or Event weapon will be far better than an SLVL 1-2 Gacha UR weapon. We also won't be covering Limited Gacha, as the supporters can be a hit or miss, for example Rinko and Murasaki were quite good, while Kirara is a bit lackluster in comparison. It is best to reroll when there is a limited banner though, or multiple gachas, since you finish the tutorial with 500 gems, which is enough for 2 discount rolls, other wise you will need to clear some levels to get up to 600 gems for two rolls of the same gacha. If you are rerolling and have questions about the limited gacha, feel free to ask in the discord.

Assist

All of the green supporters can be good, Fujibayashi Yuno is the primary target for PvP, and Kasahara Tatsumi can be quite decent. You can redeem Tekkain Kaworu from the rank 10 ticket so she is not as important to roll for.

  • PvE
    • Tekkain Kaworu

      Tekkain Kaworu is one of the best main assist supporters for PvE in the game, her active skill is guaranteed to hit all enemies on the screen, with a large amount of damage, stun, as well as one of the shorter cooldowns. Her passive skill is also decent, just providing a damage buff to critical hits.

    • Yuri Hisui

      Yuri Hisui is one of the most interesting supporters in the game. She provides a sort of glass cannon strategy where you can provide an extremely powerful 200% damage buff for 10 seconds, every 48 seconds. Unfortunately, this only really works with Emily, and Hisui is only really useful if you plan to get Emily. In addition, other just passive damage boosters tend to be better.

  • PvP
    • Fujibayashi Yuno

      Fujibayashi Yuno is the best main assist supporters for PvP, she provides invulnerability on skill, which is the current meta for PvP. Only one copy is needed to max out invulnerability time, so if you get multiple, do NOT fuse them together.

  • Both
    • Kasahara Tatsumi

      Kasahara Tatsumi is pretty decent for both PvE and PvP. She has an extremely good passive, just giving additional damage against Demons and Humans. Her lead skill is also quite good on a decent cooldown. The main utility is disarming which prevents the opponent from attacking for up to 8 seconds, providing a pseudo-invulnerability. Her main shortcoming is the short-mediumish range of the attack

Protect

None of the blue supporters in the current pool are particularly worth going for except for Amane. Their lead skills aren't very good, and their passive skills can be done by SR supporters. The only supporter worth getting is Fuuma Amane, who you can get from the rank 10 choice ticket.

  • PvE
    • Hiiragi Fuyumi

      Hiiragi Fuyumi Hiiragi is primarily used in PvE, her main skill does a decent amount of damage as well as having a large radius. One interesting thing is her skill also does a miniature time slowing effect. She can be used to buff melee damage in PvP, but other choices are typically better.

  • PvP
    • Kamiki Suzune

      Kamiki Suzune Suzune is typically used for PvP for her general damage reduction, unfortunately an SLvl 5 Nanase Mai is typically better and easier to obtain.

  • Both
    • Fuuma Amane

      Fuuma Amane has an extremely high damage buff against Machines even at SLvl 1 in both PvE and PvP, because of the relevance of Emily in PvP, Amane is extremely good. In addition, her passive can interrupt combos as well as stopping groups of enemies from overwhelming you.

Suppress

Suppress has some of the best supporters in the game currently, as well as the only SR on this list.

  • PvE
    • Fuuma Hibiki

      Fuuma Hibiki primarily used in PvE, but a PvP build can be used for her. The reason she doesn't rank higher is because she requires a Momochi weapon for a constant source of debuffs, or the character needs to have a skill that inflicts debuffs with a relatively short CD to to fully take advantage of her passive damage buff.

    • Anemone

      Anemone primarily used in PvE, but can be used in PvP against demon characters like Kurenai. Her damage boost and damage reduction works well because Demons are one of the most common enemies in the game. Unfortunately, her main skill effects don't really apply to PvE since enemies don't heal themselves.

  • PvP
    • Major

      Major Major is probably the best non invulnerability supporter for PvP in the game currently. Because of how common Emily is in Arena, having at least one Major is usually a must have. Major also synergizes well with Kurenai since her Deflecting Wind skill needs a red lead. Finally, Major can be used in PvE to delete bosses, but the targeting on skill can be a bit random. Major isn't too important to look for though, since you can get her from the rank 10 UR choice ticket.

  • Both
    • Kuonji R. Spica

      Kuonji R. Spica is the best supporter for PvP and an extremely good supporter for PvE. She provides 14 seconds on invulnerability on the lowest 49 seconds cooldown in the game. She can be used with cooldown supporters to often cheese difficult PvE content. Her skill also is guaranteed to hit every enemy in the room for a decent chunk of damage, and can even hit enemies that spawn in after the skill has been used. Her passive buffs both melee and ranged attacks as well.

    • Sanada Homura

      Sanada Homura is extremely good because at SLvl 5, she just provides a flat 30% damage boost to everything. She can be used in both PvE and PvP, and her main skill is pretty much an exact copy of Spica, just without the invulnerability. The only reason she doesn't make it into the top tier is because Homura is an SR, and therefore not as important when rerolling as some URs.
